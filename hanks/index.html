Imagine a shop where somewhere among the collection of things, there might be that one thing you were looking for all along. On Hawthorne and 34th, there’s a place where that’s still possible. That shop is Hank’s Music Exchange, run by longtime Portland gear guru Hank Failing, out of the same space as Eastside guitar repair. —Eleven Magazine
